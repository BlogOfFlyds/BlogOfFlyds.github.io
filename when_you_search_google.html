What happens when you search google


When you type in the words and click enter, the server starts searching webpages with the keyword that you typed in. That is called crawling. When the server finds a webpage with a match of the keywords, it stores the webpage. This is called indexing. Once it has a good number of websites it orders the results by relevance. The website was most relevant would appear at the top of the list. All of this happens in seconds.
